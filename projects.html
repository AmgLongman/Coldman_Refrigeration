Marine

Deep Sea Trawler Upgrade

Tema Fishing Harbor

The Challenge: Highly corrosive salt air severely degraded the existing blast freezer coils, leading to slow freezing times (12+ hours) and compromised export-grade cooling.

The Solution: Installed custom marine-grade condenser coils featuring epoxy anti-corrosion coating and upgraded compressor system.

Result: Blast freeze time reduced to 7 hours, securing premium export grading.

Agricultural

50-Ton Storage Facility

Accra Produce Market

The Challenge: 15% post-harvest loss due to inadequate temperature control during peak heat periods exceeding 32°C ambient.

Result: Stabilized core temp at 2°C, cutting product spoilage to <1%.
